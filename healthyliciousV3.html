Imagining the future of HealthyLicious

HealthyLicious is currently a dynamic platform tailored for individuals who are dedicated to improving their health and well-being.


As HealthyLicious looks to the future, it is focused on expanding its services to provide even greater value to its users. The platform plans to introduce additional features, programs, and resources to cater to a broader range of health needs and preferences. This expansion aims to offer more personalized and inclusive solutions that can adapt to the evolving needs of its user base.

Moreover, HealthyLicious is committed to fostering a sense of community and connection among its users. The platform envisions creating opportunities for users to grow and achieve their health goals alongside their friends and family. By encouraging social interaction and support, HealthyLicious aims to enhance the overall user experience, making health and wellness a shared journey. This approach not only strengthens individual commitment but also promotes collective well-being, helping users to support and motivate each other as they work towards their health and lifestyle aspirations

Problem Discovery

Unhealthy Eating Habits Demand New Solutions

Based on findings from a CDC report and Netnography*, there is a pressing need for solutions to promote healthier lifestyles. The CDC highlights that only 1 in 10 adults meet the recommended intake of fruits and vegetables, while nearly 74% are overweight or obese.

A user survey involving 30 participants focused on lifestyle-related inquiries to gain insights into individuals' health-related decisions. The survey questions aimed to understand users' eating habits. The majority of participants interviewed were aged between 18 and 35.

It leverages machine learning algorithms to suggest nutritious recipes that can be easily prepared at home, making it easier for users to make informed dietary choices and maintain a balanced diet. This makes it a convenient tool for anyone looking to improve their nutrition, manage their diet, or simply enjoy healthier meal
