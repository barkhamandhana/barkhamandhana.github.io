The National Gallery is an art museum located in London, England. It was founded in 1824 and houses a collection of over 2,300 paintings dating from the mid-13th century to the 20th century. The museum is located on Trafalgar Square and is free to enter.

The National Gallery is a popular tourist attraction and also hosts a range of educational programs, lectures, and temporary exhibitions throughout the year. It is a must-visit destination for art lovers and those interested in the history of Western art.
