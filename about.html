Currently living in San Francisco and originally from India, I believe in the power of empathy and placing the user at the center of every decision. I enjoy diving deep into user research, uncovering insights, and translating them into intuitive and visually appealing designs. I strive to create digital experiences that are playful yet effective, bringing concepts to life and enhancing the user experience.


In the past, while working with a talented team on various projects, I had the privilege of gaining a deep understanding of the product development lifecycle. This experience provided valuable insights into the importance of creating exceptional user experiences. I was actively involved in conducting user research, prototyping, and collaborating closely with developers and stakeholders to ensure seamless and user-friendly interfaces.


I specialize in turning insights and analytics into user-centric solutions that thoroughly surprise, delight, and provoke.
